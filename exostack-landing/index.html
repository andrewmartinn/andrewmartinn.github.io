FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Exostack?

Exostack is a powerful platform for seamless deployment, collaboration, and scaling of web applications. It simplifies the process of hosting, deploying, and managing your projects, offering a range of features to enhance your development workflow.

How is Exostack different from other hosting services?

Exostack stands out with its user-friendly interface, instant deployment, and a robust set of features such as global content delivery, serverless functions, and automated CI/CD pipelines. Our emphasis on collaboration, scalability, and performance sets us apart.

How do I deploy my project on Exostack?

Deploying your project is as easy as using Exostack CLI tool. You can find detailed instructions in our documentation.

How secure is my data on Exostack?

We take security seriously. Exostack implements industry-standard security measures, including SSL encryption and secure data storage. You can trust us with your sensitive data.

What benefits does the CDN integration provide?

The Content Delivery Network (CDN) ensures fast and reliable content delivery globally. Your users experience reduced latency and improved page loading times.

What programming languages are supported for serverless functions?

Exostack supports a wide range of programming languages for serverless functions, including JavaScript, Java, Python, Go, PHP and Ruby on Rails . Check our documentation for details.

How can I integrate Exostack with my existing tools?

Exostack offers extensive integrations with popular development tools and services. Explore our integrations React, Vue, Angular, Astro, Nuxt and Next to seamlessly connect with your favorite tools.

How can I get support if I encounter issues?

Our support team is here to help! Reach out to us support@exostack.com for prompt assistance. Additionally, you can explore our community forum for discussions and solutions.
